Creating your Character

Are you a Mage, Elf, Amazon, or Barbarian? Have you ever dreamed of going on an Adventure over mountains and under? Do you want to test your mettle against the most ruthless adversaries in all the lands? Then gather your friends and embark on an epic journey fraught with danger, excitement and heroism. Work together, or apart, form alliances and break them, the choices are yours in DnD. But beware! Your actions will have consequences, for good or bad on you and your allies. Come forth and forge your destiny!

Quest With Your Friends

Gather your friends and journey the lands far and wide, unlocking ancient secrets, confronting ancient prophecies, squaring off with enemies who threaten your survival.The DungeonMaster will weave a tale of such heroic proportions that they will echo through history. For good or ill, you and your alliance will make choices that affect those around you. Be the hero, be the saviour, or none of it. The fate of many will rest in your hands.

What Path Will You Take?

Adventurers can do anything that their players can imagine, but it can be helpful to talk about their activities in three broad categories: exploration, social interaction and combat. Exploration involves movement throughout the world of DnD. Social Interaction involves the conversing of players among themselves and others in the world. Combat involves characters and other creatures casting magic, swinging weapons, and maneuvering position all in an effort to defeat their adversaries. In each of these cases, the Dungeon Master informs the player of the consequences of their actions.

What Will You Encounter?

Visiting one of the great cities in the world of Dungeons and Dragons can overwhelm the senses. Voices chatter in countless different languages. The smells of cooking in dozens of different cuisines mingle with the odors of the crowded streets and poor sanitation. And the people themselves-people of varying size, shape and color, dressed in a dizzying spectrum of styles and hues-representing many different races, from diminutive halflings and stout dwarves to majestically beautiful elves, mingling with humans of varying ethnicities. The characters you encounter are truly diverse.
