Does an adventurer's sword hurt a dragon? or just bounce off its iron hard scales? Can a character swim across swim across a river, or get swept by the current? The D20 dice determines the success or failure of each character's action. Every character has ability scores. These ability scores, and the ability modifiers derived from them are the basis of every d20 roll that a player makes on a character's or monster's behalf. Ability checks, attack rolls, and saving throws are the main kinds of d20 rolls, forming the core of the rules of the game.The general steps of the game are as follows: roll and add a modifier, apply circumstantial bonus and penalties, an comparing it to a target number.
